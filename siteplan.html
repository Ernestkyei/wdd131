InfoTechHub Marketing Agency - Site Plan

InfoTechHub

InfoTechHub Marketing Agency

The name "InfoTechHub" reflects innovation and collaboration, which are core values of our marketing agency. It suggests a hub for creative and effective marketing solutions.

Site Purpose

The InfoTechHub Marketing Agency website will:

  • Showcase our marketing services (SEO, Digital Marketing, Market Research, etc.)
  • Highlight our portfolio and success stories
  • Provide a platform for potential clients to contact us
  • Offer insights and updates through a blog section

Scenarios

  • "What marketing services does InfoTechHub offer, and how can they help my business grow?"
  • "How can I contact InfoTechHub for a consultation or quote?"
  • "Can I see examples of successful campaigns InfoTechHub has managed?"

Wireframe

Mobile View

  • Vertical layout with hamburger menu
  • Logo at top center
  • Hero section with a call-to-action button
  • Scrollable sections for services, portfolio, and contact information

Desktop View

  • Horizontal navigation menu
  • Hero section with a full-width image and text overlay
  • 3-column layout for services
  • Grid layout for portfolio projects
  • Footer with contact information and social media links
